Your Questions Answered

What is a Stage 1 Remap?

Stage 1 remapping is a safe, effective ECU calibration that increases power and torque without requiring hardware changes. Read more in our ECU Remapping Guide.

Will a remap affect my fuel economy?

Often, yes — in a good way. Many drivers see improved MPG due to better torque delivery, meaning less throttle input is needed for the same performance.

What is TCU tuning and do I need it?

TCU (Transmission Control Unit) tuning optimises shift points, speeds up gear changes, and can raise torque limits. Read more about TCU tuning here.

Do you offer DPF/EGR/AdBlue solutions?

Yes — we offer software solutions for DPF, EGR, and AdBlue where legal. Learn more about our diesel services.

Is remapping safe for my car?

Yes, when carried out professionally using correct calibration methods. We work within the safe limits set by your vehicle's manufacturer.
