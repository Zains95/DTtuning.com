Why gearbox maps transform drivability — and when to pair them with Stage 1–3 engine remaps.

What TCU tuning changes

Shift timing, torque limits, clutch pressures and shift logic are refined to deliver faster, smarter gear changes and improved part-throttle behaviour.

When it’s recommended

Strongly recommended with higher-torque maps (Stage 2/3) or when stock TCU software limits torque delivery and drivability.

Real-world benefits

  • Quicker shifts and reduced flare/slip
  • Smarter shift points in Drive
  • More decisive manual mode response
  • Better torque management to support engine remaps
